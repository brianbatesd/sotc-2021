Awards & Accolades

2021 Awards and Accolades

Vancouver combines the excitement of a major metropolitan area with small-town charm and abundant recreational opportunities. We’re a fantastic place to live, work and play and the rest of the country is taking notice.

In 2021, Vancouver earned national attention as the:

No. 10 “Best Small and Midsize City to Live In” by Livability.com

No. 12 “Best Cities for Outdoor Activities in America” by Niche.com

A “Top 15 Riverwalk in the Nation” by Fodor’s Travel

Other notable accolades...

Recognized as a “Tree City USA” for the 34th consecutive year.

City vehicle fleet department named the “Best 100 Fleets in North America” and a “2021 Green Fleet” for progressive and environmentally committed fleets that go the extra mile for eco-friendly maintenance by the National Association of Fleet Administrators.

Vancouver Fire Department home fire prevention Public Service Announcements earned global recognition with a Silver Clio for creative advertising.

The City's 2021-22 budget earned the “Distinguished Budget Presentation Award” from the National Government Finance Officers Association for exceptional municipal budget preparation.
