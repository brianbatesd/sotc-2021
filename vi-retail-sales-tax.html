Thuế Bán Lẻ năm 2021

Vancouver’s retail sales tax rate in 2021 was 8.5%. Retail sales tax revenue is divided between four recipients, including the City of Vancouver.

Where does this tax revenue go?

State of Washington: 77%

City of Vancouver: 11%

C-TRAN: 8%

Clark County: 4%

$57.9 million went to the City of Vancouver in 2021

How was the City’s Portion Spent?

Police: $17.3 million (30%)

Fire: $15.6 million (27%)

Streets: $6.1 million (10%)

Parks and Recreation: $3.9 million (7%)

General Government: $3.6 million (6%)

Public Safety Support: $3.5 million (6%)

Capital Projects: $3.2 million (5.5%)

Planning & Economic Development: $2.2 million (4%)

Debt: $1.2 million (2%)

Grounds & Facilities Maintenance: $1.0 million (2%)

External Agency Support (Clark County Animal Control, Southwest Clean Air Agency, Humane Society for Southwest Washington, etc.): $0.3 million (<1%)
