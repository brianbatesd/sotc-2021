Message from the Mayor

Dear Vancouver–

Last year was yet another challenging and in many ways, also rewarding year. Together, we faced the many demands of living in the second year of a pandemic, but we seem to be turning the corner. We’re excited about our plans for 2022.

In this report, you learn about some of the City’s accomplishments from the last year. We continued to move priority projects and initiatives forward, all with the goal of improving livability. From innovative solutions to address the crisis of homelessness to progress on development efforts that will add affordable housing, new jobs and vibrancy to our growing city – we’ve got a lot to be proud of.

While this report is a look back, we are looking ahead to 2022 and beyond. As Vancouver continues to grow and community needs change, the City is working to develop a new six-year strategic plan, to serve as a road map for achieving our community’s long-term goals. You will also find details about some of the exciting projects we have planned for 2022 to address community priorities and improve livability and safety.

As a community, we’ve been through a lot together. Our shared vision, and your involvement with your community, are the reasons we are strong. It’s why we are resilient. And it’s why we continue to be a thriving and vibrant city.

As your Mayor, it is a privilege to work with the Vancouver City Council and the dedicated City employees on your behalf to improve the quality of life for all who live, work, and visit our community.

Mayor Anne McEnery-Ogle
